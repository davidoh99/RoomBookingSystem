--User Guide--

To use this program, you need to first login by going to File -> Login, or by pressing Ctrl+L.
Insert your username and password into the appropriate text fields and use the login button.

You will then be able to book a room. Choose the number of workspaces and breakout seats you would
like to have. Then, choose the options for having a smartboard, whiteboard, scanner and printer.
After doing this, click the "Book" button and the program will assign a room most suitable for 
the options you chose.
